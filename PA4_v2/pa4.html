The Ugly Duckling

Mother Duck sat waiting for her eggs to hatch.
She waited, and she waited. One day the eggs
began to crack, and out came five yellow ducklings.
There was one egg left, and Mother Duck had to sit on it for much,
much longer. Finally, the egg began to crack and out came the
last duckling. He was grey and large, and as he stood up,
the other ducklings gasped and then laughed at him.
